Why we wash bins

We started Elephant Bins because we got tired of opening our rubbish bin and dreading the smell. We thought other people might feel the same way and decided to do something about it.

"If it stinks, change it" - well we decided to clean it!

Clean bins. No fineprint.

  • Always clean with our 2-week or 4-week options.
  • Eco-friendly, biodegradable cleaning agents.
  • Water is collected and responsibly disposed.
  • Bins are cleaned on your rubbish day. No extra effort.
  • We'll text you when the job is done.
  • Add more bins. Only $3 each
  • Any wheelie bin upto 360L!
  • We accept Visa, Mastercard, Zip Pay, and bank transfer!

How it works? Simple.

  1. After the rubbish collection, we pop around and wash your bin.
  2. All waste water is collected on our truck and taken away.
  3. We sanitise bin handles after the main wash.
  4. We'll text you once your bins are clean.
  5. We'll come back in 2 or 4 weeks to do it again!

COVID-19 Response

We take your safety very seriously

  • Contactless by design. We can arrange everything over the phone, by email, or text.
  • The bin handles are fully sanitised before we leave.
  • We text you once the bins are cleaned. The next pair of hands to touch the bin is yours!
  • No need to line up! We come to you!

We take our own safety very seriously

  • 3M Full-face respirators used when cleaning bins.
  • Thick rubber gloves, avoids skin-to-bin contact.
  • High visibility clothing worn at all times.
  • Contactless service by design!
